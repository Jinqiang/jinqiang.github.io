Jinqiang Cui

I am a research scientist at Temask Laboratories, in National University of Singapore. I have finished by Ph.D under the supervision of Professor Ben M. Chen. My research focuses on simultaneous locaolization and mapping (SLAM), design and modeling of unamnned aerial vehicles (UAV).

Education

  • Ph.D.: National University of Singapore, Singapore, 2010-2015
  • Master: MEMS Lab, Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi'An, China, 2005-2008
  • Bachelor: Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi'An, China, 2001-2005
